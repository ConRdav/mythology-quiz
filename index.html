Quiz rules
Choose a difficulty to begin the quiz.
Mortal difficulty will give you 30 seconds to answer questions.
Demi-God difficulty will give you 20 seconds to answer questions.
Olympian difficulty will give you 10 seconds to answer questions.
You can only select one answer.
Once the answer is selected you can't change your choice, so be careful!
Once an answer is selected it will be automatically submitted.
There's a total of 10 questions per difficulty.
